Product Marketing

It is important to make sure that the message you want to share is synonymous with your brand or synonymous with where you want your brand to be. With Synergy Infoconnect, they provide healthcare analytics software that helps doctors track the entire care taking process and how patients are improving, and consistently reminding patients to schedule yearly, or monthly, appointments. By having patients schedule regular check-ups, doctors are able to accurately track illnesses and quickly treat symptoms.

With this in mind, I created marketing campaigns for "national awareness month" social media posts. This was to highlight any diseases or chronic illnesses that could affect people and then remind them to schedule an appointment with their doctors. I also included website links to our partnering doctors if anyone didn't have a primary care physician. These monthly campaigns increased website visitors by 135% and increased our partnering practice's online interaction by 120%.
